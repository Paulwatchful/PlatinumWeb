Welcoming, Safe, Professional.

Our Services.

Residential Care

Each guest can relax and enjoy being cared for in warm, comfortable surroundings whilst our experienced carers make them feel at home with us. Freshly prepared food is available from the kitchen prepared by our in house Chef. A range of traditional meals are available each day, and all dietary needs are taken in to account.

Respite Care

We recognise that everyone needs a break now and then, a short stay in our respite room can be enough to give you a renewed energy to live at home again. In some cases respite care may be because of a period of recovery from illness or injury, where our carers can help to make sure that the recovery is swift and well managed.

Day Care

Day Care is available between 10am and 5pm, which can be an excellent way to socialise and enjoy a day off household chores. You can enjoy a home cooked meal for lunch, and spend some time relaxing or pampering yourself in a nice hot bath, with the reassurance that our staff are on hand to look after you.

Home Cooked Food

We will take care of the food shop for you or happily transport you to the supermarket for you to do your own shopping
